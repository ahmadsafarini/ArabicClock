Reading the Dual Dial

This timepiece combines two distinct timekeeping systems into one instrument.

Main Dial (Arabic)

The large hands track Time Since Sunset.
The clock resets to 12:00 every day at Maghrib (Sunset).

Sub-Dial (Standard)

The small dial at the bottom shows your local Standard Time (e.g., what's on your phone). This complication allows you to coordinate without losing the spiritual solar context.

Golden Sun Pointer

Tracks the sun's physical position.
Left: Rising towards noon.
Right: Setting towards dusk.
